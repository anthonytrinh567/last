How It Works.

WHAT WE USUALLY NEED

Items we usually need are in very good condition. We like classics, books published within the last few years, TV shows boxset & feature film dvds, architecture, art, contemporary literature, counterculture, current textbooks published the past 3 years, fashion, graphic novels, mathematics, music books, non-fiction, philosophy, photography, recent cookbooks, Sci-fi, fantasy, young adult and much more. Records we usually buy: LPS, 45S, 12”S & 78S from the 1950s to now.

WHAT WE NEVER TAKE

We never buy books that smell like cigarettes, encyclopedias, reader’s digest, dirty, moldy, or broken books, magazines from the past 20 years, most hardcover novels, unless by an author on the most-wanted list, kids books not in like-new condition, old cookbooks, business, computer reference, health, politucs, and the Twlight series. Records we never take: most Broadway musicals, most easy listening, most polka music, titles we are overstocked with, etc.

WRITERS WE USUALLY BUY

Albert Camus, Aldous Huxley, Alfred Bester, Allen Ginsberg, Ayn Rand, Bill Bryson, Bret Easton Ellis, C.S. Lewis, Carl Jung, Carlos, Castaneda, Charles Bukowski, Chester Himes, Chuck Palahniu, Cormac Maccarthy, Dave Eggers, David Foster Wallace, David Mitchell, David Rakoff, David Sedaris, Don Delillo, Donna Tartt, Emily Dickinson, Ernest Hemingway, F. Scott Fitzgerald, Flannery O’Conner, Franz Kafka, Friedrich Nietzsche, Fyodor Dostoyevsky, G. Flynn, Gabriel Garcia Marquez, George Orwell, George R.R. Martin, H.P. Lovecraft, Haruki Murakami, and Hilary Mantel.

Sell / Trade Records.

The Last Bookstore buys, sells, and trades tons of Vinyl Records during regular business hours. The record buyer is usually at the store Monday, Wednesday, Friday, Saturday and Sunday from 10am until 5:30pm. Please call 213-488-0599 before coming in to make sure they are at the store. Please note, there is no record buyer in the store on Tuesday or Thursday. Please feel free to email our record buyer with details on what records you have, or bring them by the store. We make house calls for large collections too – please email us! We will always take a look at any records you want to sell, but most records have to be in nice condition; although we make exceptions for unique & collectible items. We pay between 1/3 and 1/2 of our sell prices. We take pride in our reputation for paying a fair amount for quality collectible records.
